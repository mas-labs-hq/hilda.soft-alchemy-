Who I Am

The Short Version

Nairobi, Kenya

I'm a 23-year-old natural health and lifestyle content creator based in Nairobi. My work sits at the intersection of traditional wisdom and modern natural living — the kind my grandmother practised long before "wellness" became a marketing word.

I create content around clean eating, natural skincare and haircare, DIY remedies, and honest product reviews. My audience comes to me because I don't hedge. If a natural product doesn't work, I say so. If something labelled "organic" is clever packaging around the same old chemicals, I break it down. That credibility took time to build and I don't compromise it for any partnership.

Which means when I recommend your product, people don't scroll past. They watch, they save, they ask where to find it — because they know I wouldn't put my name behind something I hadn't personally used and believed in.

My Mission

To make natural, chemical-free living accessible to everyday Africans — one honest piece of content at a time.

Dr Spice Organics
Organic Spice Integration & Recipe Content Campaign

Dr Spice Organics is a Kenyan brand dedicated to providing pure, chemical-free spices, herbs, and seasonings. They approached me to introduce their product range to a younger, health-conscious audience that was increasingly skeptical of conventional spice brands.

What I Delivered
  • A 4-part Instagram Reels series — "My Kitchen Switch" — replacing conventional spices with Dr Spice alternatives, showing real cooking, real reactions
  • Two original recipes featuring their products, produced as both short-form Reels and full YouTube reviews
  • Honest taste-comparison content and ingredient breakdowns (their turmeric vs. commercial turmeric, for example)
  • Behind-the-scenes cooking content on Stories with interactive polls that drove direct questions about product availability

Linkage Media Hub
Ongoing Influencer Partnership — Multiple Brand Campaigns

Linkage Media Hub is a well-established Kenyan influencer marketing agency that partners brands with micro, nano, and mid-tier influencers for authentic product marketing. I've been part of their influencer roster, executing campaigns across health, lifestyle, and FMCG categories.

What I Delivered
  • Executed paid campaigns for 4 different brands through their network — spanning food, personal care, and home products
  • Delivered content across Instagram (Reels, Stories, Carousels) and TikTok, consistently meeting brand guidelines and deadlines
  • Participated in brand briefing sessions and contributed creative input on campaign concepts and content strategy
Why This Matters
Linkage Media Hub continues to include me in their campaigns because I deliver. I understand briefs, I meet deadlines, and I create content that feels organic to my audience — not like a sponsored ad pretending to be something else. Agency repeat bookings are the most honest reference a creator can have.
